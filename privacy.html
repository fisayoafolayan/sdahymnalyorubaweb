Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

SDA Hymnal Yoruba is a free, open-source hymnal app. We do not collect personal information, require accounts, or use advertising.

Analytics

We use anonymous, cookie-free analytics to understand which features are used. No personal data is collected or stored. IP addresses are not retained.

Error Reporting

The Android app reports crashes automatically to help us fix bugs. Reports contain device model, app version, and the error — no personal data.

Local Data

Your preferences, favorites, and cached hymns are stored on your device only. They never leave your device and are removed if you clear app data.
